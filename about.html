Who We Are

About Luminet Electric Engineering

Built on Trust, Wired for Excellence

Luminet Electric Engineering is a Kuala Lumpur-based electrical contracting company specialising in residential, commercial, and light industrial electrical works. We have been serving clients across the Klang Valley with a commitment to safety, quality, and reliability.

From a simple wiring job to a full-scale renovation project, our team brings the same level of professionalism and attention to detail to every engagement. We are licensed and fully certified, ensuring all our work complies with Malaysian electrical standards and regulations.

Whether you're a homeowner looking to upgrade your electrical system, a business needing CCTV and alarm installation, or a developer requiring complete electrical fit-out for a new project — Luminet is your trusted partner.
