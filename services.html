What We Offer

Our Services

Electrical Wiring & Installation

Complete electrical wiring solutions for new builds, renovations, and rewiring projects. All work is carried out to Malaysian Standards (MS IEC 60364) by certified electricians.

  • New residential & commercial wiring
  • Rewiring of existing properties
  • Socket, switch & light point installation
  • Outdoor & garden lighting wiring
  • 3-phase power supply installation
CCTV Installation

Keep your home or business secure with professional CCTV installation. We supply, install, and configure complete surveillance systems tailored to your needs.

  • HD & IP camera supply and installation
  • Indoor and outdoor camera systems
  • DVR/NVR setup and configuration
  • Remote viewing via smartphone
  • Maintenance and troubleshooting
Alarm System Installation

Protect your property with reliable alarm systems. From basic intruder alarms to full smart-home integrated security setups, we handle the complete installation.

  • Intruder & burglar alarm systems
  • Motion sensor installation
  • Door & window magnetic sensors
  • Siren and alert module setup
  • GSM & smart alarm integration
DB Box & Switchboard

Professional installation, upgrading, and maintenance of distribution boards and switchboards for residential and commercial properties.

  • New DB box installation
  • MCB and ELCB replacement
  • Consumer unit upgrades
  • Earthing & bonding works
  • Load balancing & circuit labelling
Renovation Electrical Works

Supporting renovation projects of all sizes with complete electrical works coordination. We work closely with contractors, interior designers, and property owners.

  • Full electrical fit-out for renovations
  • Lighting design & installation
  • Air-conditioning power points
  • Kitchen & bathroom electrical works
  • Office and retail fit-out
Maintenance & Repair

Fast-response electrical maintenance and repair services. Whether it's a tripped breaker, faulty wiring, or a complete system fault, our team is ready to help.

  • Electrical fault diagnosis
  • Wiring repair & replacement
  • Tripped MCB & ELCB rectification
  • Power failure troubleshooting
  • Preventive maintenance scheduling
